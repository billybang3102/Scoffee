Revolutionary Packaging Innovation

At Scoffee, we've created something truly revolutionary: premium coffee grounds in packaging that completely dissolves when brewed.

Our innovative packaging is made from seaweed and our grounds coated with your choice of xylitol or sugar that dissolves in hot water, leaving behind zero waste. Even better, the seaweed acts as a natural fertilizer for your plants!

Zero Waste

Our coating completely dissolves, leaving absolutely no waste behind. Also, the seaweed helps plants around the area.

Eco-Friendly

Made from sustainable seaweed that doubles as a natural plant fertilizer.

Convenient

Simply drop 1 pod into hot water - no measuring, no waste.

How It Works

Our revolutionary dissolving coffee coating makes brewing delicious coffee easier and more sustainable than ever.

1

Choose Your Coffee

Select from our range of premium, sustainably sourced coffee grounds in our innovative seaweed packaging.

2

Drop In Hot Water/Insert Into Machine

Simply drop the entire pod into hot water or a machine. No need to open it or measure - just drop it in!

3

Watch It Dissolve

Your choice of coating (xylitol or sugar) dissolves first, releasing the coffee grounds.

4

Enjoy & Compost

Enjoy your delicious coffee! Remember to stay brewed with purpose.
